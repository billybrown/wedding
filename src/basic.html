Travel Arrangements

The Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport (MSY) is located approximately 16 miles from downtown New Orleans. Taxis and Airport Shuttles to both hotels are readily available. Taxi rides from the airport to the CBD (downtown central business district, very near the hotels) average $33.00. Shuttle service is available for $20.00 but requires advanced booking.

Please visit FlyMSY for additional information.

Activities

  • Get beignets atCafe du Monde.
  • Take the PRC Christmas Home Tour in the historic Lower garden district.
  • Tour the nearby plantations of Oak Alley, Laura's Pantation or Longue View Home and Gardens.
  • Ride the St. Charles Avenue streetcar.
  • Visit the neighborhoods of Uptown, and the Garden District.
  • Stroll down Magazine Street and visit boutiques and antique shops.
  • Go to Julia Street to enjoy the New Orleans Art Scene.
  • Explore the French Quarter with a Daquiri in hand.
  • Take a Mississippi River Boat ride from downtown to Audubon Zoo and Park.
  • Visit the D-Day Museum, the New Orleans Museum of Art, NOMA Sculpture Garden, the Contemporary Art Center, the Audubon Aquarium of the Americas, and the new Insectatarium.
