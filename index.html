Travel Arrangements

The Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport (MSY) is located approximately 16 miles from downtown New Orleans. Taxis and Airport Shuttles to both hotels are readily available. Taxi rides from the airport to the CBD (downtown central business district, very near the hotels) average $33.00. Shuttle service is available for $20.00 but requires advanced booking. Visit FlyMSY for additional information.

Introducing our new

Family Crest

To celebrate our two families coming together we made a crest. It pictures a blue heron, a bird native to Louisiana (and Vogel means 'bird' in german), standing inbetween two twin oaks (the Brown family home has two giant oak trees).

The top of the sheild has 8 points representing the families that came before us (Vogel, Rooney, Fitzpatrick, ???, Brown, Wallace, Crockett, Meadows).

Our house motto is, "The Wedding is coming."
